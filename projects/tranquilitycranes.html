Tranquility Cranes is a mobile app that helps people manage stress, anxiety, and mental health. It offers guided exercises, activity suggestions, schedules, and community forums for support. The mascot, Terry Crane, provides encouragement, while a unique egg feature reminds users to care for themselves. With a calming design and soft blue colors, the app promotes peace and self-care.

Hi-Fidelity Prototype

Target Audience

  • Individuals experiencing stress and anxiety

    They need tools to manage their mental well-being.

  • People seeking self-care

    Require mindfulness practices to improve their daily routines.

  • Young adults and students

    Stuck facing academic pressure and life transitions.

Solutions

  • Guided Coping Exercises

    Step-by-step techniques to manage stress and anxiety.

  • Personalized Activity Suggestions & Schedules

    Helps users build healthy self-care habits.

  • Community

    A safe space for users to connect, share experiences, and receive support.

Features

  1. Suggest Activities Quiz

    Based on your mood, preferred environment, activity intensity, and social preference (introverted or extroverted

  2. Guided Exercises

    Simple breathing exercises that are easy to follow.

  3. Live Public Chat Room

    Working chat room to communicate with others with relatable experiences.

  4. Dark Mode Support

    Features a dark mode to minimize eye strain and improve accessibility, especially for users with visual sensitivities.

User Persona

User personas help identify and address the needs of the target audience, guiding design decisions to keep the app practical, user-friendly, and relevant.
